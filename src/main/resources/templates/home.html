Welcome to Dr. Doe's Personal Page

Dr. Jane Doe is certainly a lot more than her cancer. She is very active as a mentor as a youth IT instructor at the Boys' and Girls' Club. She upcycles thrifted garments into children's clothes and stylish outfits for herself. Dr. Doe has an active speech schedule that has taken her to Egypt, Japan, China, and across the U.S.A.
